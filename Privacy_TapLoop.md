# Privacy Policy for Tap Loop

Effective date: December 21, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Tap Loop ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use Tap Loop, an Android automation application.

By using Tap Loop, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

Tap Loop is designed to function **without requiring personal information or user accounts**. We do not collect:

- Names, emails, or other personal identifiers
- Location data
- Contacts or messages
- Browsing history

The app only stores **local configuration data** on your device, such as:

- Action flows you create
- App package names
- Action parameters and settings
- Logs for debugging purposes

This data **never leaves your device**.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

All data stored by Tap Loop is used exclusively for:

- Enabling automation features
- Persisting your settings and flows
- Providing visual debug tools within the app

We **do not share or sell your data** to third parties.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored **locally on your device**.  
- We implement standard security practices to protect your app configuration data.  
- Even if the app is uninstalled, some cached settings may remain on your device.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

Tap Loop does **not integrate external analytics or advertising SDKs**. The app functions fully offline.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Tap Loop is **not intended for children under 13**. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
